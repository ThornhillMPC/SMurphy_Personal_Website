STEPHEN MURPHY

    FILM | BOOKS | COMEDY

Stephen Murphy is a FILM DIRECTOR, WRITER and COMEDIAN from Toronto.

His most recent film, Dennis is in the Bathtub, was an official selection at both the After Dark Film Festival and the Toronto Independent Film Festival, and is now available on Vimeo. Steve is currently developing his first feature film.

His book Get Rich or Die Trying: A Humor Collection was published by Abbott Press and is available at Amazon and other fine retailers.
